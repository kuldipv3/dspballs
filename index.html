About DSP

Five decades of focus on precision balls

Dular Steel Products (DSP) began manufacturing balls in the 1970s and has grown into a specialist producer of precision grade balls across multiple metals and special materials. Production is guided by recognised international ball standards, with every order aligned to drawings or detailed discussions about the end application.

From common carbon and chrome steel balls to stainless, brass, copper, silver and tungsten carbide, DSP also supports niche requests such as odd sizes and small runs that are hard to source in the open market.

  • Precision balls in steels, non-ferrous metals and hard materials like tungsten carbide
  • Capability to supply unusual diameters and short-run special sizes
  • Support for ball plug gauges and related precision components
